Find information about products and services helps you discover and explore digital products and services across the Department for Education.

What you can do

Use this service to:

  • search and filter DfE products and services
  • view detailed information about each product including contacts and responsibilities
  • explore technical components and categories for each service
  • find contact details for product owners and delivery teams

Data sources

The service displays information from the DfE Configuration Management Database (CMDB).
