About {{ taxonomy | lower }} categories

{% if taxonomy == 'Phase' %}

Phase categories describe the stage a product or service is at in the service delivery lifecycle, from initial exploration through to live service and eventual decommissioning.

{% elif taxonomy == 'Business area' %}

Business area categories represent the organisational portfolio or department responsible for delivering a product or service.

{% elif taxonomy == 'Type' %}

Type categories describe the nature and functionality of the service, such as whether it's transactional, informational, or provides data collection capabilities.

{% elif taxonomy == 'Channel' %}

Channel categories describe the delivery methods through which services are provided to users, such as web, mobile apps, or face-to-face interactions.
